## 4. THE SYNTHESIS FLOW

### The Synthesis Flow

Most systems collect. This one compounds.

Log one gratitude, one lesson, one moment — 30 seconds at the bottom of your Day Page. Each entry dates itself and flows into the system automatically.

**Daily (30 seconds)**
Capture what mattered. Three buttons. Done.

**Weekly**
The system compiles your entries into a reflection view. Pick your top lesson. Answer prompts like "Where did I fall short?" and "What surprised me most?"

**Monthly**
Weekly lessons aggregate into a Monthly Battle Plan. Patterns surface. Strategy forms.

**Yearly**
An automatic Year in Review — built entirely from what you already entered. Top lessons, highlights, and moments from every month. One page.

You did not do extra work. You just showed up daily. The system kept the rest.
